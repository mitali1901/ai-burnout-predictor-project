Wellness Tips

Evidence-based tips to maintain digital wellness and prevent burnout:

Sleep

Prioritize Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep. Poor sleep significantly impacts cognitive function and burnout risk.

Breaks

Take Regular Breaks

Use the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.

Movement

Stay Active

Regular physical activity reduces stress and improves mental clarity. Even 10-minute walks help.

Pro Tip

Use the scenario buttons (Healthy, Moderate, Burned Out) to see how different lifestyle choices impact your wellness score. This helps you understand cause and effect relationships.
